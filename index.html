Why European Portuguese?

Portugal sounds different.
This app teaches you that.

Most Portuguese apps teach Brazilian Portuguese — perfectly fine if you're heading to São Paulo. But if you're visiting Lisbon, Porto or the Algarve, you need European Portuguese. The accent is different, the vocabulary is different, and even the grammar has its own character.

· · · ✦ · · ·

Every word, tip and example in this app reflects how Portuguese is actually spoken in Portugal — from ordering a bica in a Lisbon café to catching the comboio to Porto. No Brazilian spellings, no Brazilian idioms. Just the real thing.
